Numbered Parts Description

  1. External Jaws: Measure the external dimensions of an object.
  2. Internal Jaws: Measure the internal diameter of hollow cylinders or tubes.
  3. Depth Blade: Measure the depth of holes or slots.
  4. Main Scale (cm): Fixed scale graduated in centimeters and millimeters.
  5. Main Scale (inch): Fixed scale for inch measurements.
  6. Vernier Scale (mm): Sliding scale providing readings in millimeters.
  7. Vernier Scale (inch): Sliding scale for inch measurements.
  8. Lock Screw: Locks the movable jaw after measurement.
